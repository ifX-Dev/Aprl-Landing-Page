Why Us?

EARTH-FRIENDLY

We aim at offering apparel made from the most ecological materials favoring natural and recycled materials for their exceptional qualities. Our roots run deep (literally) in organic farming, we use sustainable crops, organic farms, and chemical-free dyes to ensure our fibers are supporting Mother Earth from seed to tee.

LOCAL MANUFACTURING

All ApÆrl products are manufactured thanks to know-how craftsmen in France or Portugal in order to produce sustainably and promote local industries. All ApÆrl products are made in West Africa, the maximum distance traveled between ApÆrl processors and customers is 1,750 km.

AFRO-FUSION

We aim at changing the narrative of fashion by creating everyday functional clothing that celebrate the African History and Heritage. We create value by empowering people to wear a brand that celebrates them, and offer quality clothes that have an intrinsic value.
